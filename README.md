## About Me

I build software, AI/ML, and data-driven systems that solve practical problems. I recently completed my Master's in **Information Systems Management** at **Carnegie Mellon University**, and my work sits at the intersection of software engineering, AI, analytics, and product thinking.

- Currently working as a **Research Assistant at Carnegie Mellon University** on large-scale **Google Ads bidder and auction data analysis**, studying auction dynamics, bidder behavior, response quality, and marketplace patterns
- Previously worked on CMU research involving **large-scale U.S. copyright registration analysis** and **mobile safety behavior analytics**
- Experience across **software engineering, AI/ML, analytics, and consulting** through CMU research, PwC India, and capstone work with A.T Kearney
- Interested in **AI systems, machine learning, data-intensive applications, analytics, and product-minded engineering**
- Published work in **IEEE** and **Springer** on applied AI projects
- Reach me at **aravinda992@gmail.com** or **aboovara@andrew.cmu.edu**
